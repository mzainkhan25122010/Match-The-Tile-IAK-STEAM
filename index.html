How to Play

Match The Tile is a memory-based puzzle game where you need to find matching pairs of tiles within a time limit.

Game Rules

  • Click on tiles to flip them and reveal their symbols
  • Flip two tiles at a time to try and find a matching pair
  • If the tiles match, they will remain face up
  • If they don't match, they will flip back after a short delay
  • Find all pairs before time runs out to win the game
  • Complete levels to unlock more challenging games

Scoring System

  • Each matched pair: 100 points
  • Each second remaining: 10 bonus points
  • Each move made: -5 points
  • Level completion bonus: Level Ã— 200 points
  • Timer bonus multiplier: 30s=1x, 60s=1.5x, 90s=2x

Tips for Success

  • Start with the easier levels to learn tile patterns
  • Remember tile positions as you flip them
  • Plan your moves to minimize unnecessary flips
  • Practice with different timer settings to improve your speed
